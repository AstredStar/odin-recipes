Nightshade Stew

Home

Easy but delicious stew

Nightshade family plants make for great stew. The key is in good quality ingredients

I cook this dish at least once a week!

Ingredients

  • Eggplant, 1
  • Cut Bell peppers, 3, don't use green peppers
  • Tomato paste, 1 tbsp
  • Diced Tomatoes, 4
  • Diced Onions, 2
  • Chopped Garlic, 2-4 cloves
  • Scotch bonnet pepper, 1
  • Chopped basil (optional)
  • Cubed meat (optional), 300-500g

Steps

  1. Dice eggplant, salt and let liquid drain for 1hr.
  2. Fry eggplant in pot on medium heat, remove eggplant nad set aside on paper towels to drain oil.
  3. Add oil and fry meat in pot then set aside in a covered bowl.
  4. Add oil and fry onions in the pot. Add wine or water to deglaze pot.
  5. Once onion begins to turn soft, add bell peppers.
  6. Once peppers begin to soften, Add scotch bonnet and garlic.
  7. When garlic is toasted, add Tomato paste, spices, salt and pepper.
  8. When the tomato paste begins to darken, add tomatoes. Try to crush the tomatoes while they cook.
  9. Once the tomatoes are soft and mushy, add eggplant and meat.
  10. Let simmer for 10-30 minutes. Add water as needed. Add basil when there is 1-2 minutes left
