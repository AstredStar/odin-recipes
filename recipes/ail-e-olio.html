Simple but tasty pasta

Ail e olio is a very simple traditional italian pasta dish. The key is in good quality ingredients

I cook this dish at least once a week!

Ingredients

  • garlic, 6 cloves
  • chili pepper, 1
  • olive oil, 1/2 cup
  • parmesan oregano, 1/3 cup grated
  • spaghetti, 500g
  • lemon juice, 20ml
  • parsley, 1/3 cup chopped

Steps

  1. Cut garlic and pepper into thin slices.
  2. Toast garlic and pepper in olive oil on low heat.
  3. Salt pasta water and cook pasta to instructions. Save 1 cup pasta water at the end.
  4. Mix garlic and oil with pasta water to desired consistency.
  5. Drain pasta, add parmesan and mix thoroughly.
  6. Then add the garlic sauce and mix untill pasta is well coated with sauce.
  7. Finally, add salt, pepper and parsely.
